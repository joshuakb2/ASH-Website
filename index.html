Atheists, Skeptics & Humanists at the University of Texas at Dallas

An Affiliate of the Secular Student Alliance

Welcome to the official page of Atheists, Skeptics, and Humanists at UT Dallas, also known as ASH.

We meet every Friday night at 6pm in the SSA 14.510 room. Come join us for presentations, discussions, games, and more! This group is open to anyone interested, regardless of religious or philosophical beliefs. After every meeting we have dinner at various restaurants.
